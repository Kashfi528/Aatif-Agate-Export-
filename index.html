Manufacturing & Export

How we prepare wholesale orders.

This process page keeps manufacturing and packing details inside the menu instead of stretching everything on the homepage.

1

Raw Stone Selection

Material is selected based on product category, finish and buyer requirement.

2

Cutting & Shaping

Rough material is shaped into towers, spheres, angels, pendulums or other forms.

3

Polishing

Each item is polished to create a smoother, retail-ready finish.

4

Sorting

Products are sorted by category, appearance, finish and assortment suitability.

5

Quality Check

Visual and finish checks help maintain buyer confidence before packing.

6

Secure Packing

Products are grouped, protected and carton packed depending on category and order.

7

Export Dispatch

Orders are prepared for dispatch from India with inquiry support available through Canada.
