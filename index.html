Where Every Piece Begins

A craft rooted in one city, kept in one family.

Khambhat has been known for its agate work for generations — a tradition of cutting, tumbling and polishing stone that's been passed down through skilled hands. Aatif Agate Export has been part of that tradition for more than 25 years.

We don't outsource. Every tumbled stone, heart, pyramid, geode, pendulum, bracelet and tower that leaves our workshop is cut, shaped and finished under our own roof — which is how we keep quality consistent, whether the order is a single box or a full container.
